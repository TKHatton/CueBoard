# CueBoard — Demo Script

> This is the choreography for the hackathon demo.
> Practice this flow until it's muscle memory.

---

## Setup (Before Demo Starts)
- [ ] Logitech MX Creative Console connected and recognized
- [ ] CueBoard plugin loaded in Logitech Actions app
- [ ] Zoom open (or simulation mode active)
- [ ] Console showing Page 1 (Live Controls)
- [ ] Export folder empty (so the export feels fresh)

---

## Act 1: "It Just Works" (Page 1 — Live Controls) ~30 seconds

**What you say:**
> "Every day, millions of people fumble through Zoom menus to do simple things.
> CueBoard puts the most common controls at your fingertips — literally."

**What you do:**
1. Press **Mute** → button shows "Muted" → press again → shows "Live"
2. Press **Camera** → toggles video
3. Press **Record** → red indicator appears on button
4. Press **Screen Share** → share starts
5. **Use the dial** → rotate through reactions → press to send a thumbs up

**What judges see:** Instant control. No mouse. No menus. Physical and satisfying.

---

## Act 2: "Now You're the Operator" (Page 2 — Operator Mode) ~45 seconds

**What you say:**
> "But CueBoard isn't just for attendees. If you're running a meeting or workshop,
> you need tools that go beyond mute and unmute."

**What you do:**
1. Switch to **Page 2**
2. Press **Mute All** → "This silences the whole room with one touch."
3. Press **Spotlight Speaker** → "Now everyone sees who's presenting."
4. **Set a timer:** Rotate dial to 5 minutes → press dial to start → countdown appears
5. Press **Admit from Waiting Room** → "Someone just joined late — one button, they're in."

**What judges see:** Authority. Control. This feels like a production tool, not a toy.

---

## Act 3: "The Feature Nobody Else Has" (Page 3 — Meeting Intelligence) ~60 seconds

**What you say:**
> "AI tools record everything. CueBoard lets YOU decide what matters —
> in the moment, with a physical button, without breaking your flow."

**What you do:**
1. Switch to **Page 3**
2. Use **dial** to select "Action Item" type
3. Press **Flag Moment** → "Sarah needs to send the Q3 report" → counter shows "1"
4. Use **dial** to select "Decision" type
5. Press **Flag Moment** → "We approved the vendor switch" → counter shows "2"
6. Press **Flag Moment** again → counter shows "3"
7. Press **Clear Last** → "Oops, didn't mean that one" → counter back to "2"
8. Press **Export Summary** → Markdown file generated

**What you say while export happens:**
> "And just like that — a structured meeting summary. Action items, decisions,
> timestamped and organized. No AI hallucinations. No 10-page transcript.
> Just what YOU flagged as important."

**Show the exported Markdown file on screen.**

---

## Closing (~15 seconds)

**What you say:**
> "CueBoard turns the MX Creative Console into something every meeting host
> has been waiting for. Three pages. Physical controls. Real-time intelligence.
> This is what a meeting control surface should feel like."

---

## Emergency Fallback (If Something Breaks)

If Zoom crashes or hardware disconnects:
- Switch to **simulation mode** — everything still logs to console
- The demo still works, it just shows simulated output instead of live Zoom
- Don't apologize — just say "Let me show you how this works under the hood"

## Timing Target
- Total demo: **~2.5 minutes**
- Leave time for Q&A
- Don't rush Page 3 — that's the closer
